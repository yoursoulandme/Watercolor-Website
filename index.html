Realistic Watercolor Food Painting Tutorial

How to Paint Flavorful Food Illustration with Watercolor


Food is such an integral part of our lives and our culture. We eat to celebrate important moments, discover new things, bring ourselves comfort, and evoke familiar memories. Watercolor is perfect for food illustration and is the medium illustrator Enya Todd uses to create everything from editorial work for publications to packaging designs. She has worked for clients like British Airways and shares her work with over 45K followers on Instagram.



Who is this online course for?

This course is for anyone with an interest in illustrating their favorite food using watercolors.

Requirements and materials

No previous experience is required. In terms of materials, you will need a pencil and eraser, watercolor paint, paint brushes, watercolor paper, paper towels, and other materials specified by the teacher in the course.
